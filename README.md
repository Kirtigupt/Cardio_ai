# Cardio AI

## Intelligent Cardiovascular Patient Monitoring and Risk Assessment System Using Explainable AI

Cardio AI is a full-stack healthcare application designed to monitor patient vital signs in real time and provide AI-powered cardiovascular risk assessment. The system analyzes patient health data, detects anomalies, classifies risk levels, and assists medical professionals in making faster and more informed decisions.

---

## Problem Statement

Cardiovascular diseases remain one of the leading causes of mortality worldwide. Traditional monitoring systems often fail to provide intelligent risk prediction and real-time decision support. Cardio AI addresses this challenge by integrating AI-driven analysis with patient monitoring.

---

## Objectives

* Monitor patient vital signs in real time.
* Predict cardiovascular risk levels using AI models.
* Detect anomalies and generate alerts.
* Provide explainable AI insights for medical staff.
* Generate downloadable medical reports.
* Improve clinical decision-making efficiency.

